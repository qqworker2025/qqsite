Scholarship Announcement 2025

Nurturing Talent, Igniting Futures

The QuantaQuest Foundation, officially established in 2025, builds upon years of dedicated informal support. Our mission is to empower talented students from disadvantaged backgrounds, enabling them to pursue higher education and realize their dreams. Beyond financial aid, QuantaQuest provides a supportive community where students can cultivate essential skills, enhance their knowledge, and gain valuable experience for their careers and lives.

Scholarship Type

Full scholarship for the entire duration of university/high school studies.

Number of Awards

35-60 scholarships available for the 2025 academic year.

Scholarship Value

Up to 10,000,000 VND per academic year.

Application Criteria

Applicants must meet the following criteria to be considered.

Family Background

Students from difficult family circumstances lacking the economic means to pursue university/high school education. QuantaQuest will conduct direct home and local verification.

Academic Performance

Must have achieved at least "Advanced Student" status for three consecutive years in high school. Priority is given to students with higher achievements.

Award Conditions

  • Successful admission to a university/high school is required.
  • The student must not be receiving a full or regular scholarship from other organizations.
  • Scholarships are contingent on maintaining "Good" academic and conduct performance.

Application Process & Timeline

Follow these steps to apply for the QuantaQuest 2025 scholarship.

Online Registration

Students must apply online via the official registration link. Ensure all required information and documents are attached. The application window is from July 1, 2025, to August 10, 2025.

Results Announcement

The results of the registration round will be announced on the QuantaQuest website by September 30, 2025. Selected students will also receive an official notification letter.

Final Submission

Selected applicants must submit hard copies of their application, university/high school admission notice, and a signed commitment letter to formally receive the scholarship.

Apply Online Now

Applications open July 1, 2025.
