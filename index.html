Our Mission

QuantaQuest Foundation is a non-profit social foundation committed to advancing education. We provide scholarships, awards, and support to high-achieving students from lower secondary to postgraduate levels in Ninh Binh province and across Vietnam, fostering learning and contributing to human resource quality for our homeland.
