About QuantaQuest Foundation

Learn about our journey, our values, and the people driving our mission forward.

Our Mission & Philosophy

Empowering Vietnam's future through education.

At QuantaQuest, our core mission is to create a transformative impact on education in Vietnam. We believe that access to quality education is the key to unlocking human potential and fostering sustainable development. Our philosophy is rooted in three main principles:

  • Accessibility: Ensuring that financial constraints do not hinder talented and passionate students from pursuing their dreams.
  • Excellence: Promoting a culture of high achievement and continuous learning, recognizing and rewarding academic merit.
  • Community: Building a supportive network of students, mentors, and partners dedicated to contributing to the nation's growth.

Our Vision

To be a leading catalyst for educational advancement in Vietnam, nurturing generations of innovative thinkers, compassionate leaders, and skilled professionals who will shape a prosperous future for the homeland.

The QuantaQuest Journey: Igniting Dreams

Key milestones in our history.

2022

A Spark from Compassionate Hearts

In 2022, amidst the lingering economic scars of the post-COVID-19 pandemic, two dedicated sons of Ninh Binh province, Pham Ngoc and Tran Tung, felt a deep sense of concern. They witnessed a poignant reality: many students, despite possessing extraordinary academic talent and an unyielding will to learn, faced immense economic hardship, putting their dreams at risk of being unfulfilled. Unwilling to see such potential fade, Ngoc and Tung decided to ignite the first spark. Using their personal finances and unwavering conviction, they quietly took action, personally seeking out and awarding the very first scholarships. Each scholarship wasn't just material support; it was a beacon of light, fueling the determination of these bright, resilient students, a testament that their commitment to a brighter future had truly begun.

2023

Steady Steps with Passionate Friends

Inspired by the initial success and the heartwarming stories of those they had helped, 2023 saw Ngoc and Tung's efforts touch the hearts of a close-knit group of about 10 longtime friends. These passionate individuals, sharing a common desire to serve their homeland's education, readily contributed their own finances and time. Together with Ngoc and Tung, they personally sought out, assessed, and delivered scholarships and essential support to outstanding students who, despite their brilliance, lacked favorable conditions. This collective effort transformed initial personal endeavors into a warm and effective movement of shared purpose.

2024

Cultivating the Foundation's Vision, Expanding Impact

In 2024, building on the successes and experiences gained from their small group activities, Ngoc, Tung, and their dedicated collaborators realized that to help even more students—especially those talented individuals overcoming adversity to attend top universities—a grander vision and a formal non-profit organization were essential. This marked the pivotal period when the idea of the QuantaQuest Foundation truly began to take root and flourish, driven by the goal of fostering a stronger educational environment, not only in Ninh Binh province but also reaching further across the nation.

2025

QuantaQuest Foundation Officially Established

After a journey of immense effort, persistence, and unwavering dedication, 2025 saw the QuantaQuest Foundation officially receive its license and recognition as a non-profit organization. This was a sweet reward for years of nurturing a vision, from personal concerns to the collective action of the community. This milestone not only marked a crucial legal step but also affirmed QuantaQuest's long-term commitment to empowering dreams, helping students overcome all economic barriers to become exceptional scholars at leading universities, and thus write new chapters of success and contribute meaningfully to society.
